About us- your camping getaway

Come camp with us. You'll feel right at home!

Our campsites have the greatest views, whether near the lake or deep in the forest, there is a place for everyone. As our region is more temperate in the spring and summer, it is best to camp here at these times. The campsite is open all-year-round if you are looking for chilly winter evenings by the fire under the stars.

Plan your trip

Only a drive away. Every route is the scenic route.

Three convenient hightways lead to our beautiful redwood forest. Highway 105, Highway 88, and State Route 3 are all convenient ways to get to our hiking trails, education center, and campgrouds.Depending on the length of your stay, you can pay for a day pass or a discounted multi-day parking permit. Parking is not far from the campsites or hiking trails, making your trip even easier to plan.
